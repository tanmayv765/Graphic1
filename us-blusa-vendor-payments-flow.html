Open Questions & Gaps

7 open items from interviews — HIGH severity (Q1–Q3) · MEDIUM severity (Q4–Q7)

High Q1
What is the payment volume and frequency for US vendor payments?
Neither monthly volume nor cycle frequency is documented in the interviews. This information is required for process design and capacity planning post-NetSuite.
Confirm with Lisette Roa: how many vendor payments are processed per week/month, and on which days does the payment run occur?
High Q2
Is there an approval or dual-authorisation step before or after payment execution in First Financial?
No approval gate or segregation of duties is documented — potential controls gap. Dual-authorisation requirements and signatory authority are undocumented.
Ask Lisette Roa and Finance leadership: what authorisation controls exist in First Financial Bank portal? Who has signatory authority, and are payments reviewed before execution?
High Q3
Will NetSuite enable bulk payment file upload to First Financial Bank, or will payments remain one-by-one?
The manual one-by-one entry is the primary pain point shared across all regions. No bulk upload or bank integration is documented for the future state — the most critical efficiency gap.
Escalate to NetSuite implementation team and First Financial Bank: is a bulk payment file (e.g., NACHA/ACH) supported? Is this in scope for the NetSuite go-live?
Medium Q4
What is the end-to-end time per payment cycle?
Hours spent per cycle is documented as "NOT DOCUMENTED" in the post-NetSuite workflow metadata. Required for ROI analysis of any automation initiative.
Ask Lisette Roa to time-track the next two payment runs and report back total hours spent on data entry, review, and execution.
Medium Q5
How are Mexico entity payments handled at the bank level — same First Financial portal or separate banking relationship?
Lisette handles Mexico AP but the banking arrangement (same First Financial portal or separate bank) is not documented. Currency (USD vs MXN) also unclear.
Ask Lisette Roa: is Mexico's bank the same First Financial account, or a separate banking relationship? Are Mexico payments executed from a USD or MXN account?
Medium Q6
What is the Bill.com migration status post-NetSuite?
Plan to migrate to NetSuite noted but not confirmed as completed. Status is ambiguous per migration map. Some vendors/clients may still be routing payments through Bill.com.
Confirm with Finance ops team: is Bill.com still active post-NetSuite go-live? Which vendors/clients are still using it, and what is the migration timeline to NetSuite?
Medium Q7
What is the payment process for Blink (BL Digital Inc.) — same First Financial Bank workflow or separate?
Blink uses a different accounting system (Xero current / NetSuite future) and holds the Google GMP contract. The bank payment process for Blink is not separately documented.
Ask Lauren Reiser: how are Blink vendor payments executed? Same First Financial Bank portal as Blusa, or does Blink have a separate bank account and payment workflow?
